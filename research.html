MiniFluo-UV and SeaExplorer glider
(Cyr, Tedetti, Goutx, et al.)


During the whole year 2016 I was working at the Mediterranean Institute of Oceanography in Marseille on the EU project NeXOS. My role was to test and validate a new glider-compatible optical sensor (the MiniFluo-UV) for measurements of dissolved organic matter.
Poster presented at the Ocean Sciences meeting in New Orleans (February 2016).




Trapped diurnal tides - Rockall Bank
(Cyr, van Haren, et al.)


Internal swashing on the slopes of Rockall bank capture by high resolution thermistor chain.
Arrival of a deep bore shoaling on the slopes of Rockall bank (sequence from a moored thermistor chain, 12-h period).
