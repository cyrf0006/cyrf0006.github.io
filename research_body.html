Research projects

SeaExplorer gliders
Since January 2016, I am working with the French-built glider Sea Explorer. Along with contributing the toolbox by adding the SeaExplorer case, my role is to focus on dissolved organic matter dynamics in the Northwestern Mediterranean Sea. To do so, I will use observations from a new optical sensors, the MinifluoUV.
